Blood Culture Interpretation

  • True pathogen indicators: Multiple positive bottles, same organism, short time to positivity (<24h), S. aureus/GNR/Candida
  • Likely contaminant: Single bottle positive, coag-neg Staph, Corynebacterium, Cutibacterium, Bacillus (in immunocompetent)
  • Time to positivity: <14h suggests high-grade bacteremia; important for endocarditis
